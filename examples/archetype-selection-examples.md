# Archetype Selection Examples

These examples show how to use verified online references to decide drafting baselines.

## Example A: `to C` AI chat with mobile verification

- Product pattern:
  - user opens a public site
  - logs in by mobile phone
  - chats directly with an AI assistant
  - no enterprise certification
- Select:
  - `provider_to_c_mobile_login_ai_assistant`
- Baseline source:
  - `千问`
- Reason:
  - this is the cleanest verified direct-to-consumer AI assistant archetype in the current reference pool

## Example B: SMB AI website builder with content and customer-service functions

- Product pattern:
  - user buys an AI website-building service
  - product includes workspace, online customer service, and content generation
  - buyer may be a small business but not a fully enterprise-certified admin-only customer
- Select:
  - `provider_to_c_site_builder_with_workspace`
- Baseline source:
  - `万小智AI建站`
- Reason:
  - verified product page shows purchase, renewal, workspace, and business-use signals that pure chat products do not cover

## Example C: AI builder console with APIs

- Product pattern:
  - user enters through a console
  - product focuses on model or app building
  - operator acts more as a platform than a content service provider
- Select:
  - `supporter_console_platform`
- Baseline source:
  - `阿里云百炼`
- Reason:
  - verified agreement text explicitly covers API and integration style opening

## Example D: Enterprise agent platform with digital employees

- Product pattern:
  - enterprise purchases the service
  - admin and employee structure exists
  - product helps build or deploy agents or digital employees
- Select:
  - `supporter_enterprise_agent_platform`
- Baseline source:
  - `AgentOne`
- Reason:
  - verified public positioning is enterprise-first and deployment-oriented

## Example E: Agent product with tool and skill execution

- Product pattern:
  - assistant can call tools or skills
  - can process files or webpages
  - may execute actions
  - local or sandbox permissions are part of the risk model
- Select:
  - `work_ai_claw_high_privilege`
- Baseline sources:
  - `JVS Claw`
  - `钉钉悟空`
  - `ArkClaw`
- Reason:
  - verified legal and product materials explicitly discuss automated execution, tools, webpages, files, and safety boundaries
